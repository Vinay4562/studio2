Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some common questions about our services.

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for bridal services and 1-2 weeks for party makeup to ensure your preferred date and time slot.
Do you provide makeup trials?
Yes, we offer makeup trials for bridal packages to ensure you love your look before the big day. Contact us to schedule one!
What products do you use?
We use high-quality, professional-grade products from brands like MAC, Huda Beauty, and Kryolan, tailored to your skin type and preferences.
Do you travel for appointments?
Yes, we offer travel services within Hyderabad for an additional fee. Please contact us for details and pricing.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 30 days prior are fully refundable. Within 30 days, a 50% fee applies, and no refunds are given within 7 days.
